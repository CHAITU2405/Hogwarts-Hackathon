  • All team members must be currently enrolled students.
  • Each team must consist of 3-4 members.
  • Participants must register before the deadline.

2. Code of Conduct

  • All participants must maintain professional and respectful behavior throughout the event.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or any form of inappropriate conduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants must follow all event rules and guidelines.

  • All code must be original work created during the hackathon period.
