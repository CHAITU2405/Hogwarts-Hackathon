Educational Decree No. 24

"Rules of Conduct for the upcoming Tournament"

By Order of the Ministry
The High Inquisitor of Hogwarts
Fellowship Formation
  • Teams must consist of 3 to 4 wizards or witches.
  • Teams are requested to carry the extension cords with them.
  • All Wizards/Witches must be present in the Great Hall (Venue) for the duration of the hackathon.
  • Wizards/Witches ID cards will be given at registeration desk at the starting of the hackathon.
  • Cross college teams are also allowed to participate in the hackathon.
  • If any GVPT(RUSHIKONDA) students are found participating, they will be removed from the team.
  • GVPT(RUSHIKONDA) STUDENTS ARE NOT ALLOWED TO PARTICIPATE
Code of Conduct
  • Problem statements are released on 05/12/2025 morning 06:00 AM-06:00 PM.Once opted can't be changed.
  • Keep the castle clean! Vanish your rubbish into the nearest bin.
  • Respect your fellow wizards. Duelling is only allowed in the form of code optimization.
  • Respect organizers and volunteers and follow the instructions given by them.
  • Incase of inconvenience, report to the nearest organizers or volunteers.
Submission & Judging
  • All projects must be submitted to the Repository of Secrets (GitHub) before the sand in the hourglass runs out.
  • Judge’s decisions are final, as per the rules of the Ministry.
